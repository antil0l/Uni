$$x-x_{0}=(v_{0}cos{\theta_{0}})t$$
# حرکت قائم
$$v-v_{0}=v_{0y}t-\frac{1}{2}gt^{2}$$
$$= (v_{0}sin\theta_{0})t-\frac{1}{2}gt^2$$
$$v_{y}=v_{0}sin\theta_{0} - gt$$
$$v^{2}_{y}=(v_{0}sin\theta_{0})^{2}-2g(y-y_{0})$$
# معادله مسیر
$$y=(tan\theta_{0})x-\cfrac{gx^2}{2(v_{0}cos\theta_{0})^2}$$
